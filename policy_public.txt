GOLIATH NATIONAL BANK - EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (PUBLIC)

1. PTO POLICY: All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year, accruing at 1.66 days per month.
2. REMOTE WORK: Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week (Tuesday/Thursday) with manager approval.
3. OFFICE HOURS: Standard core operating hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time, Monday through Friday.
4. DRESS CODE: The dress code is "Business Casual." Jeans are permitted on Fridays only.
5. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT: Travel expenses under $50 do not require a receipt, but must be logged in Concur within 30 days.
6. SECURITY BADGES: Badges must be worn visibly at all times while on corporate premises. Lost badges incur a $25 replacement fee.
7. IT SUPPORT: The IT Helpdesk is available 24/7. Call +1-415-010-4357 for immediate assistance with hardware issues.
8. SOCIAL MEDIA: Employees must not post confidential company information on public social media platforms. Refer to the Non-Disclosure Agreement.
9. PARENTAL LEAVE: Primary caregivers are eligible for 16 weeks of paid leave; secondary caregivers are eligible for 6 weeks.
10. HOLIDAYS: The company observes 10 federal holidays, plus two "Floating Holidays" to be used at the employee's discretion.
11. ETHICS HOTLINE: Anonymous reporting of unethical behavior can be done via the third-party portal at ethics.goliathbank.com.
12. GYM STIPEND: All employees receive a $50/month wellness stipend added to their second paycheck of the month.
